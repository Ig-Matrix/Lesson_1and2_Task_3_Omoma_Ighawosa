My NYSC Experiences

Follow me as I recount my time serving as a Corp Member in Awka, Anambra State.

My Experiences were majorly in Teaching, CDS Leadership and I4GxZuri Training.

Teaching

I served in a popular secondary school in Umuokpu.The name of the school is Community Secondary School, Umuopkpu. I was assigned to the Physics department.The classes I taught were SS1 and SS2

My activitie as a teacher were in two fold which were explanationsand Marking Scripts

Explanation

I took time explaining the topics to the students. Most of them had poor science backgrounds and I had to utilize several examples and visible illustrations to preach the Gospel of Physics to them. I entertained questions and also asked them questions aimed at sharpening their minds and improving their cognitive abilities. Initially, it was one sided with me doing the all the talking but as time went and I piqued their interest, they began to follow along well. I really relished this aspect of the job.

Marking Scripts

I must admit I didn't enjoy this part of the job. I tught the full arm of SS1 and there were 6 arms with each arm having an estimate of 50 students. This left me with the humongous task of marking and recording the scripts of 300 students every time they wrote tests and exams or had a project. Over time, I learnt some hacks and with the help of my HOD, I was able to acclamatize well.

CDS

CDS stands forCommunity Development Service , and it is an integral part of the National Youth Service Corps (NYSC) program in Nigeria. During the service year, all corps members are expected to participate in CDS activities aimed at improving the host community's socioeconomic development. CDS groups are formed based on corps members' interests and skills, and they work together on various projects such as education, health, environment, agriculture, and entrepreneurship. CDS programmeis a vital component of the NYSC scheme. It provides a platform for Corps Members to contribute to the development of their host communities while also developing their skills and improving their employability.

During my service year, I belonged to the Digital Onboarders CDS group and served as the President.

The major activities carried out by the CDS are Google my Business(GMB) profiling and Digital skills sensitizations

Google my Business(GMB) Profiling

Google My Business (GMB) profiling is the process of creating and managing a free online business listing on Google. GMB is a tool provided by Google that enables businesses to manage their online presence on Google Search and Google Maps.

With GMB profiling, businesses can provide important information to potential customers, including their business name, address, phone number, website, business hours, photos, and reviews. This information is displayed prominently on Google Search and Maps, making it easier for customers to find and contact the business.

During my tenure, we profiled over 400 businesses on Google.

Digital Skills Sensitization

Digital skills sensitization refers to the process of increasing awareness and understanding of digital technologies and their potential benefits, as well as promoting the development of digital skills among individuals or organizations. It involves educating people on the importance of digital literacy and equipping them with the necessary knowledge and skills to effectively use digital tools and technologies.

I4GxZuri Training.

The I4GxZuri Training was an intensive 3 months internship on fullstack development. It was this training the kickstarted my career as a Software Developer. The technologies covered included HTML, CSS and Javascript for Frontend and Django, Python for Backend.

The training was in two parts; Front end web development and Backend Web development

Frontend Web Development

During the front end training, I learnt how to build simple responsive websites using HTML, CSS and Javascript I learnt correct syntax and usage of elements, properties and functions in the various technologies.

For example, I learnt how to use the pre tag in html. Unlike the p tag, the pre tag is used to display text exactly as it appears in the HTML code.
